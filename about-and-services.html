About Us & Services

What We're All About

Our motto is very simple "Its Your World We Just Design It"

CsickMedia is a small design and development studio located in Atlanta, Georgia. Our goal is not only to create unique, elegant designs, intelligent navigation, user friendly, and functional websites, but also provide clients the type of service that will help strengthen their business. Through the years we have worked with clients that are active in movies, music, sports, technology, and small businesses.

We specialize in designing and creating rich High Quality Websites, Logos, CD/DVD Artwork, Myspace Pages, Flyers, Business Cards, Posters, Brochures, Signages, Adverts, PowerPoint Designs, and more. Not only do we serve for Web Design purposes, but for Printing as well.

Our services can be used for personal, presentational, small, and large businesses. Our team has the expertise, knowledge, and dedication in creating stunning presentations with special effects and/or Flash experience which are beyond any clients expectations.

CsickMedia is a company that is driven with passion and attention to details with the knowledge to deliver simple and complex products for everyone. Each layout design is completed with dedicated passion, hard work, and special attention to the smallest detail.

Our expert software and programing knowledge includes extensive work with Adobe Flash, Photoshop, Illustrator, Indesign, CSS, XML, xHTML, JavaScript, Sony Vegas, Final Cut, Pro-Tools, and more.
